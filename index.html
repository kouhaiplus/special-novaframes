FAQ

What services do you offer?

We specialize in VFX, 3D animation, and compositing. Our expertise covers a wide array of visual services.

How long do projects take?

Project timelines vary depending on the scope and complexity. We work closely with clients to establish realistic schedules.

Do you do post-production?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post-production services, including color correction and final mastering.

How does pricing work?

Our pricing is project-based, reflecting the unique requirements of each undertaking. Contact us for a detailed quote.

What software do you use?

We use industry-standard software such as Autodesk Maya, Houdini, and Nuke to deliver top-quality results.

About Our Studio

Nova Frames Studios is a cutting-edge VFX studio dedicated to crafting breathtaking visual experiences. Our mission is to push the boundaries of what's possible in visual effects, creating stunning imagery that captivates audiences.

Our team is composed of highly skilled artists and technicians, each a master in their respective fields. We bring a wealth of experience and passion to every project, ensuring exceptional results.

We pride ourselves on our collaborative approach, working closely with our clients to bring their visions to life. From concept to final delivery, we are committed to excellence and innovation.
